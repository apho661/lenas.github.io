About

Beauty, fresh, looking nails. Lena's was created in 2004, hoping to make people feel pretty in and out. The name is actually come from one of the most important people in my life. When someone feels beautiful from the outside, maybe then they can start feeling pretty from the inside. If you want to have beautiful looking nails, come find us! We offer more than just painting nails! Come to us for nails, massage, feet, and facial!
